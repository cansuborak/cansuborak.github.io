Alibaba.com Sales Performance: Comprehensive Analysis and Visualization Report



Project Goal

The Sales Analysis serves as a critical tool for the Alibaba.com e-commerce platform, providing in-depth year-over-year evaluations of Total Sales and Total Profit, along with detailed insights into product subcategories and trend visualizations to enhance strategic planning, marketing, and inventory management.

Project Set Up

The initial phase of the project prioritized data cleaning to ensure precise and accurate analysis. This critical step involved the elimination of duplicate entries and the establishment of calculated fields to facilitate year-over-year comparisons. Refined and cleansed the raw data using SQL queries and stored procedures to ensure data integrity and reliability. Furthermore, they created calculated fields to enhance visualizations, such as percentage differences in profit and sales, along with average KPI profits.

Analysis


The analysis of the company's annual sales from 2021 to 2023 revealed a consistent upward trajectory in revenue across the three years. In 2021, the company began its review with sales totaling $471,000. This figure rose significantly to $609,000 in 2022, evidencing substantial growth. The upward trend persisted into 2023, when sales peaked at $733,000, marking the highest point of the period. This data not only underscores the company's expanding market presence but also delineates the annual highs and lows in its financial performance.
