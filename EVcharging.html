EV Charging Trends: Key Insights and Observation



Project Goal

The objective of the EV Charging Patterns Analysis project is to examine charging behaviors across major cities, identify peak usage periods, and derive insights into energy consumption and user demographics. This analysis aims to support the development of sustainable transportation by enhancing the accessibility, efficiency, and convenience of EV charging solutions, thereby accelerating the transition to clean energy and reducing reliance on fossil fuels.

Project Set Up

The project setup involved a systematic data preparation process to ensure the accuracy and reliability of the analysis. Initially, the data was subjected to a comprehensive cleaning procedure, including the removal of duplicate entries and the resolution of inconsistencies. Missing values were identified and replaced using statistical methods, such as mean or median imputation, to maintain the dataset's integrity.

Subsequently, datasets from multiple sources were merged based on key identifiers to create a unified and comprehensive view of EV charging patterns. New variables were generated, including time-based categorizations (e.g., morning, afternoon, evening, night), to facilitate temporal trend analysis. Outliers were identified and addressed to prevent skewed results. This structured and refined dataset provided a robust foundation for analyzing energy consumption, user demographics, and peak charging behaviors.

Conclusion

The EV Charging Patterns Analysis provided valuable insights into charging behaviors, energy consumption, and user demographics. By systematically preparing and analyzing the data, key trends were identified, including peak charging times, user preferences, and energy usage across different charger types. These findings offer actionable recommendations for improving the accessibility, efficiency, and convenience of EV charging infrastructure. Ultimately, this analysis supports the transition to sustainable transportation by enabling data-driven decisions that reduce reliance on fossil fuels and promote clean energy adoption.
