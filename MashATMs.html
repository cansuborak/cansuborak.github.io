Mash ATMs Sales Analysis


Project Goal


      The Sales Analysis serves as a critical tool for the ATM company, providing in-depth year-over-year evaluations of Total Sales, Total Profit, and along with detailed insights into product subcategories and trend visualizations to enhance strategic planning, marketing, and inventory management.

Set Up


      The initial phase of the project prioritized data cleaning to ensure precise and accurate analysis. This critical step involved the elimination of duplicate entries and the establishment of calculated fields to facilitate year-over-year comparisons. Refined and cleansed the raw data using SQL queries and stored procedures to ensure data integrity and reliability. Furthermore, they created calculated fields to enhance visualizations, such as percentage differences in profit and sales, along with average KPI profits.
